Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: January 2026 🌍 GDPR & CCPA Compliant ✉️ privacy@pinviral.com

Plain English Summary

  • We don't sell your data to anyone, ever
  • Product images you upload are processed by Google Gemini AI — not stored by us
  • We use browser fingerprinting only to enforce the 1-device free trial limit
  • Payments are handled entirely by Stripe — we never see your card number
  • You can export or delete all your data at any time from Account Settings
  • We use no third-party tracking or advertising cookies

1 Who We Are

PinViral AI ("PinViral", "we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered Pinterest marketing platform. Our service is accessible at pinviral.com.

For privacy enquiries contact: privacy@pinviral.com

2 Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — collected when you subscribe to a paid plan via Stripe
  • Product images — uploaded by you to generate AI pin visuals. Sent to Google Gemini API for processing and not stored on our servers
  • Browser fingerprint — a hash derived from canvas rendering, AudioContext output, and navigator properties. Stored in your browser (localStorage + IndexedDB) to enforce the one-device free trial policy
  • Usage data — images generated, plan type, credits used. Stored locally in your browser
  • Payment data — processed entirely by Stripe. We receive only a customer ID and subscription status — never your card details
  • Pinterest OAuth token — if you connect Pinterest, your access token is stored locally in your browser and used only to publish pins on your behalf

3 How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and operate the PinViral service
  • To enforce free-plan limits via browser fingerprinting
  • To process payments and manage your subscription via Stripe
  • To generate AI images and marketing copy via Google Gemini API
  • To publish pins to Pinterest on your behalf (only when you connect your account)
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, plan changes) — no marketing emails without your consent

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party.

4 Browser Fingerprinting

To enforce our one-device free trial policy, we generate a browser fingerprint using:

  • Canvas API rendering hash
  • AudioContext floating-point output
  • Screen resolution, color depth, timezone, and platform
  • Navigator hardware concurrency and device memory

This fingerprint is stored in your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB only. It is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

By using PinViral you consent to this fingerprinting as described in our cookie consent banner.

6 Cookies & Local Storage

PinViral does not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

We use your browser's local storage and IndexedDB to store:

  • Your session data (plan, credits remaining)
  • Browser fingerprint (free trial enforcement)
  • Stripe keys you manually enter (never transmitted to our servers)
  • Pinterest access token (if you connect Pinterest)
  • Your cookie consent preference

You can clear this data at any time using Account Settings → Delete Account & Data or by clearing your browser's site data.

7 Data Retention

All session data is stored locally in your browser. PinViral does not maintain a cloud database of user content. Stripe retains billing records as required by financial regulations (typically 7 years). Google Gemini processes images transiently — refer to Google's DPA for their retention policy.

8 Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

Access Request a copy of data we hold about you
Rectification Correct inaccurate personal data
Erasure Delete your account and all associated data
Portability Export your data as JSON from Account Settings
Withdraw Consent Opt out of fingerprinting by not using the free trial
Opt-out of Sale We never sell data — this right is already fulfilled

To exercise any right email privacy@pinviral.com. We respond within 30 days. For immediate data deletion use Account Settings → Delete Account & Data.

9 Children's Privacy

PinViral is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with data please contact privacy@pinviral.com.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via in-app notification or email (if we have your email on file) with at least 14 days notice. Continued use of PinViral after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

The current version is always available at pinviral.com/privacy.

11 Contact & Complaints

For privacy questions: privacy@pinviral.com

If you are in the EU and unsatisfied with our response you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.
